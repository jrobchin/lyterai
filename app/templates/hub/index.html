Welcome to LyterAI!

by Jason Chin

Lyter AI is an independent research project with a goal in exploring the implementation of machine learning models on webservers. A current field of research is optimizing machine learning models for runtime and minimal hardware. In the near future, I envision the possibility of distributing machine learning models to be seamlessly implemented into projects by developers who are not experienced in machine learning.
